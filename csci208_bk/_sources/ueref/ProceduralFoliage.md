# Procedural Foliage

1. From the **Main Menu** open the **Edit** menu then click on **Editor Preferences**.

   [![Editor Preferences](images/02-pfs-editor-prefs.jpg)](https://docs.unrealengine.com/5.0/Images/building-virtual-worlds/open-world-tools/procedural-foliage/02-pfs-editor-prefs.png)

   Click image for full size.

1. Inside of the Editor Preferences **right - click** on the **Experimental** section.

1. Enable the Procedural Foliage option by clicking on the checkmark box next to the word **Procedural Foliage**.

   [![Editor Preferences](images/03-pfs-editor-preferences.jpg)](https://docs.unrealengine.com/5.0/Images/building-virtual-worlds/open-world-tools/procedural-foliage/03-pfs-editor-preferences.png)

   Click image for full size.
